Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical consulting engagement last?

Engagements typically range from 2-12 weeks depending on scope. We start with a discovery phase to assess your needs and create a custom timeline.

Do you offer remote consulting?

Yes, all of my consulting services are available remotely. We communicate via video calls, emails, and collaborative tools.

What if my team uses different tools than you specialize in?

No problem! The principles of good QA are universal. I can adapt my approach to your existing tools and help optimize your current setup.

Can you help with ongoing support after the engagement?

Absolutely! I offer hourly consulting and retainer packages for teams that need continued support and mentorship.

How do you measure the success of a consulting project?

We establish clear KPIs during discovery - things like test coverage increase, bug detection improvement, CI/CD efficiency, and team confidence with automation.
